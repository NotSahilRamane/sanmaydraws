About Me

Hey! I'm Sanmay Rajguru. I'm 18 years old and I'm from Mumbai, India. 
I loved sketching and painting since my childhood. In 2019 I started an Instagram page for my drawings. Later I even started a small business of commissioned portrait sketches. I draw portraits of famous Movie/Series characters, Animals, landscapes. Sometimes I even draw anime characters and mandala art.
